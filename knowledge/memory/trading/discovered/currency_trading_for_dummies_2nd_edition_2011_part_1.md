## 2026-02-24

Part I: Trading the World’s Largest Financial Market This part introduces you to the global forex market and gives you an idea of its size and scope. You find out who the players are and what their styles and motivations are. I also look at the impact of other financial markets on currencies. This part is also where I cover the nitty-gritty of currency trading conventions and tools so you know exactly how and what you’re trading.
