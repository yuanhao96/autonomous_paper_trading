Chapter 8, “Risk and Money Management.” Countertrend Trading A countertrend trading style makes money when markets are not trending by using a strategy that is the opposite of trend following. Instead of buying when markets make new highs, traders who use countertrend strategies sell short at prices close to the same new highs, counting on the fact that most breakouts of new highs do not result in trends.
