Chapter 3 details the Price Alternation Principle, which explains why some indicators work well in certain environments and then fail in others. The principle also lays the groundwork for range-trading, mean reversion trading strategies, which give rise to breakout strategies that can lead to large profits at the exact genesis of a new move in the market.
