# WQ-178: Relative volume surge times proximity to daily low

## Formula
(volume / sma(volume, 20) - 1) * (1 - (close - low) / (high - low + 1e-8))

## Interpretation
Relative volume surge times proximity to daily low. Positive when volume is high AND price closed near the low -- capitulation/selling climax buy signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_sma | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
