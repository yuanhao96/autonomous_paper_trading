# WQ-094: Signed volume times daily return, normalized by price

## Formula
where(delta(close, 1) > 0, volume, -1 * volume) * delta(close, 1) / close

## Interpretation
Signed volume times daily return, normalized by price. Positive on up days with volume, negative on down days.

## Parameters
None

## Category
volume_price
