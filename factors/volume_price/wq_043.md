# WQ-043: Daily price change scaled by relative volume

## Formula
volume / sma(volume, 20) * delta(close, 1)

## Interpretation
Daily price change scaled by relative volume. Large moves on high relative volume get amplified -- volume-confirmed momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_sma | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
