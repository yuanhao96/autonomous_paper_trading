# WQ-085: Product of volume rank and return rank

## Formula
ts_rank(volume, 20) * ts_rank(delta(close, 2), 15)

## Interpretation
Product of volume rank and return rank. High when both volume and recent returns are relatively high -- trend confirmation.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_rank_window | 20 | [10, 40] |
| return_rank_window | 15 | [7, 30] |

## Category
volume_price
