# WQ-060: Deviation from price SMA, scaled by relative volume

## Formula
-1 * (close - sma(close, 10)) * (volume / sma(volume, 20))

## Interpretation
Deviation from price SMA, scaled by relative volume. Negative sign = mean reversion on high-volume deviations.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| price_sma | 10 | [5, 20] |
| vol_sma | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
