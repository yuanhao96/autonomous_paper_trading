# WQ-139: Daily return weighted by negative open-volume correlation

## Formula
-1 * correlation(open, volume, 10) * delta(close, 1)

## Interpretation
Daily return weighted by negative open-volume correlation. Contrarian when opens and volume are positively correlated.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| corr_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
volume_price
