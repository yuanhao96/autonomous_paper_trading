# WQ-080: Volume change rate times negative price change rate

## Formula
delta(volume, 5) / delay(volume, 5) * (-1 * delta(close, 5) / delay(close, 5))

## Interpretation
Volume change rate times negative price change rate. Positive when volume rises but price falls (accumulation) or volume falls and price rises.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
volume_price
