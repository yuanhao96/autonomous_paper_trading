# WQ-150: Rate of change of close x volume product

## Formula
(close * volume - delay(close, 1) * delay(volume, 1)) / (delay(close, 1) * delay(volume, 1) + 1e-8)

## Interpretation
Rate of change of close x volume product. Captures joint price-volume momentum.

## Parameters
None

## Category
volume_price
