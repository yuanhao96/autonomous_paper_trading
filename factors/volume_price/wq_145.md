# WQ-145: Relative volume ratio (5-day avg vs 20-day avg)

## Formula
sma(volume, 5) / sma(volume, 20) - 1

## Interpretation
Relative volume ratio (5-day avg vs 20-day avg). Positive when recent volume exceeds historical -- volume surge indicator.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| fast_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| slow_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
