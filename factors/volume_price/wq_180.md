# WQ-180: Volume change rate minus price change rate over 3 days

## Formula
delta(volume, 3) / delay(volume, 3) - delta(close, 3) / delay(close, 3)

## Interpretation
Volume change rate minus price change rate over 3 days. Positive when volume is increasing faster than price -- accumulation signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 3 | [1, 6] |

## Category
volume_price
