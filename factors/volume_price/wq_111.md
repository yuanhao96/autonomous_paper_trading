# WQ-111: Short vs long SMA of Chaikin-like Accumulation/Distribution

## Formula
sma(volume * (close - low - high + close) / (high - low + 1e-8), 11) - sma(volume * (close - low - high + close) / (high - low + 1e-8), 20)

## Interpretation
Short vs long SMA of Chaikin-like Accumulation/Distribution. Crossover signal -- positive when recent accumulation exceeds longer-term.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| fast_window | 11 | [5, 22] |
| slow_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
