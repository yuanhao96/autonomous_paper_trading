# WQ-155: Daily change in the product of smoothed volume and smooth...

## Formula
sma(volume, 13) * sma(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 13) - delay(sma(volume, 13) * sma(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 13), 1)

## Interpretation
Daily change in the product of smoothed volume and smoothed returns. Volume-momentum acceleration.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 13 | [6, 26] |

## Category
volume_price
