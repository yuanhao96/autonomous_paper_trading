# WQ-168: Negative relative volume

## Formula
-1 * volume / sma(volume, 20)

## Interpretation
Negative relative volume. Contrarian signal -- bearish when volume spikes (potential panic/euphoria).

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volume_price
