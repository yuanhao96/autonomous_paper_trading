# WQ-117: High volume rank x low price-range rank x low return rank

## Formula
ts_rank(volume, 32) * (1 - ts_rank(close + high - low, 16)) * (1 - ts_rank(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 32))

## Interpretation
High volume rank x low price-range rank x low return rank. Signals when volume is high but price action is subdued -- potential breakout setup.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_rank | 32 | [16, 64] |
| price_rank | 16 | [8, 32] |
| ret_rank | 32 | [16, 64] |

## Category
volume_price
