# WQ-084: Daily price change confirmed by volume direction

## Formula
sign(delta(close, 1)) * sign(delta(volume, 1)) * delta(close, 1)

## Interpretation
Daily price change confirmed by volume direction. Zero when volume moves opposite to price.

## Parameters
None

## Category
volume_price

## Source
WorldQuant Alpha#084 (Kakushadze 2015)
