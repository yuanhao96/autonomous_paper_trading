# WQ-102: Ratio of avg(volume x |return|) to avg(volume) x avg(|ret...

## Formula
sma(volume * abs(delta(close, 1)), 10) / (sma(volume, 10) * sma(abs(delta(close, 1)), 10) + 1e-8) - 1

## Interpretation
Ratio of avg(volume x |return|) to avg(volume) x avg(|return|). Measures co-movement between volume and absolute returns -- positive when volume clusters on big-move days.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 10 | [5, 20] |

volume_price
