# WQ-068: Daily return weighted by relative volume and normalized d...

## Formula
(volume / sma(volume, 15)) * delta(close, 1) * (high - low) / close

## Interpretation
Daily return weighted by relative volume and normalized daily range. Triple confirmation: volume + momentum + range expansion.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_sma | 15 | [7, 30] |

volume_price
