# WQ-011: Williams %R-like measure weighted by volume

## Formula
(close - low - (high - close)) / (high - low + 1e-8) * volume

## Interpretation
Williams %R-like measure weighted by volume. Measures where close sits in daily range, amplified by volume.

## Parameters
None

## Category
volume_price
