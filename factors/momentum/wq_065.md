# WQ-065: Ratio of average daily change to current daily change

## Formula
sma(delta(close, 1), 6) / delta(close, 1)

## Interpretation
Ratio of average daily change to current daily change. Smoothed momentum relative to spot momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 6 | [3, 12] |

## Category
momentum
