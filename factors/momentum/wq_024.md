# WQ-024: 5-day price change plus average daily change over days 5-10

## Formula
delta(close, 5) + (delay(close, 5) - delay(close, 10)) / 5

## Interpretation
5-day price change plus average daily change over days 5-10. Combines short and medium momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| short_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| long_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
momentum
