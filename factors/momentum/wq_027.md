# WQ-027: Deviation of price from its 6-day moving average

## Formula
sma(close, 6) / close - 1

## Interpretation
Deviation of price from its 6-day moving average. Positive when price is below average (reversion signal within momentum context).

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 6 | [3, 12] |

## Category
momentum
