# WQ-053: Change in the high-close to close-low ratio over 9 days

## Formula
-1 * delta((high - close) / (close - low + 1e-8), 9)

## Interpretation
Change in the high-close to close-low ratio over 9 days. Captures shifts in intraday price positioning.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 9 | [4, 18] |

momentum
