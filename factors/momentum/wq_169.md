# WQ-169: Difference between 10-day and 5-day average daily change

## Formula
sma(delta(close, 1), 10) - sma(delta(close, 1), 5)

## Interpretation
Difference between 10-day and 5-day average daily change. Momentum trend slope.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| slow_window | 10 | [5, 20] |
| fast_window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
momentum
