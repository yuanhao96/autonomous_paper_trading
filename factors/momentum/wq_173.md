# WQ-173: Linearly-decayed 5-day returns over 10 days

## Formula
decay_linear(delta(close, 5) / delay(close, 5), 10)

## Interpretation
Linearly-decayed 5-day returns over 10 days. Recent momentum weighted more heavily.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| return_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| decay_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
momentum
