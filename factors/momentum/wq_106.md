# WQ-106: 20-day simple return

## Formula
close / delay(close, 20) - 1

## Interpretation
20-day simple return. Medium-term momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
momentum
