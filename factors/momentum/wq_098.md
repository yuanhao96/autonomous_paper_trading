# WQ-098: 5-day rate of change of the 10-day SMA

## Formula
delta(sma(close, 10), 5) / delay(sma(close, 10), 5)

## Interpretation
5-day rate of change of the 10-day SMA. Smoothed momentum acceleration.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 10 | [5, 20] |
| roc_window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
momentum
