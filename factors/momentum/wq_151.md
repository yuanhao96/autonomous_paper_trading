# WQ-151: Price minus 20-day SMA

## Formula
close - sma(close, 20)

## Interpretation
Price minus 20-day SMA. Classic trend deviation -- positive above the average.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
momentum
