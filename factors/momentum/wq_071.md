# WQ-071: 24-day price deviation plus linearly-decayed time-series ...

## Formula
(close - sma(close, 24)) / sma(close, 24) + decay_linear(ts_rank(close, 16), 4)

## Interpretation
24-day price deviation plus linearly-decayed time-series rank. Combines trend and rank momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 24 | [12, 48] |
| rank_window | 16 | [8, 32] |
| decay_window | 4 | [2, 8] |

## Category
momentum
