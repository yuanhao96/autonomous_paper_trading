# WQ-152: 5-day average of yesterday's 12-day average return

## Formula
sma(delay(sma(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 12), 1), 5)

## Interpretation
5-day average of yesterday's 12-day average return. Double-smoothed lagged momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| inner_window | 12 | [6, 24] |
| lag | 1 | [1, 2] |
| outer_window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
momentum
