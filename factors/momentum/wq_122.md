# WQ-122: Triple-smoothed log price (like TRIX base)

## Formula
sma(sma(sma(log(close), 3), 3), 3)

## Interpretation
Triple-smoothed log price (like TRIX base). Very smooth trend indicator.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| smooth_window | 3 | [1, 6] |

## Category
momentum
