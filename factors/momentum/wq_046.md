# WQ-046: Difference between prior momentum (days 10-20) and recent...

## Formula
(delay(close, 20) - delay(close, 10)) / 10 - delta(close, 10) / 10

## Interpretation
Difference between prior momentum (days 10-20) and recent momentum (last 10 days). Momentum deceleration/acceleration.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback_near | 10 | [5, 20] |
| lookback_far | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
momentum
