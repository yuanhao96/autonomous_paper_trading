# WQ-146: 5-day average of daily returns

## Formula
sma(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 5)

## Interpretation
5-day average of daily returns. Smoothed short-term momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
momentum
