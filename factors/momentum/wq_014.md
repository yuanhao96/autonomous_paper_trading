# WQ-014: 3-day price change weighted by price-volume correlation

## Formula
-1 * delta(close, 3) * correlation(close, volume, 10)

## Interpretation
3-day price change weighted by price-volume correlation. Signals momentum confirmed or denied by volume relationship.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| delta_days | 3 | [1, 6] |
| corr_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
momentum
