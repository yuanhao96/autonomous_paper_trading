# WQ-031: Price deviation from 12-day SMA plus linearly-weighted 2-...

## Formula
(close - sma(close, 12)) / sma(close, 12) + decay_linear(delta(close, 2) / delay(close, 2), 5)

## Interpretation
Price deviation from 12-day SMA plus linearly-weighted 2-day returns.

| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 12 | [6, 24] |
| return_lookback | 2 | [1, 4] |
| decay_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
