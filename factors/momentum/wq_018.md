# WQ-018: Combines intraday range volatility (normalized) with 10-d...

## Formula
-1 * stddev(abs(close - open), 5) / (close - open) + delta(close, 10) / close

## Interpretation
Combines intraday range volatility (normalized) with 10-day price momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| vol_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| mom_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
momentum
