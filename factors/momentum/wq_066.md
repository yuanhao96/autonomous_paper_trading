# WQ-066: Price deviation from 6-day mean as percentage

## Formula
(close - sma(close, 6)) / sma(close, 6)

## Interpretation
Price deviation from 6-day mean as percentage. Classic mean-deviation momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 6 | [3, 12] |

## Category
momentum
