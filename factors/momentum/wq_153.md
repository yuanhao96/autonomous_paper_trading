# WQ-153: 3-day vs 12-day SMA difference

## Formula
sma(close, 3) - sma(close, 12)

## Interpretation
3-day vs 12-day SMA difference. Short-term trend crossover signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| fast_window | 3 | [1, 6] |
| slow_window | 12 | [6, 24] |

## Category
momentum
