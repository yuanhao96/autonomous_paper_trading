# WQ-175: Average directional gap tendency

## Formula
sma(abs(high - delay(close, 1)) - abs(low - delay(close, 1)), 10)

## Interpretation
Average directional gap tendency. Positive when highs gap up more than lows gap down -- bullish volatility bias.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
volatility
