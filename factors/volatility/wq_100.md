# WQ-100: Negative coefficient of variation over 5 days

## Formula
-1 * stddev(close, 5) / sma(close, 5)

## Interpretation
Negative coefficient of variation over 5 days. Bearish when volatility is high relative to price level. Favors low-volatility regimes.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
volatility

WorldQuant Alpha#100 (Kakushadze 2015)
