# WQ-004: Negative time-series rank of today's low over 9 days

## Formula
-1 * ts_rank(low, 9)

## Interpretation
Negative time-series rank of today's low over 9 days. Low when today's low is at the top of its range -- sells strength in lows, buys weakness.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 9 | [4, 18] |

## Category
volatility
