# WQ-161: Smoothed 10-day price range normalized by current close

## Formula
sma(ts_max(high, 10) - ts_min(low, 10), 5) / close

## Interpretation
Smoothed 10-day price range normalized by current close. ATR-like measure of normalized volatility.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| range_window | 10 | [5, 20] |
| smooth_window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
volatility
