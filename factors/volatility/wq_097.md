# WQ-097: Ratio of return volatility to average absolute return, mi...

## Formula
stddev(delta(close, 1), 5) / sma(abs(delta(close, 1)), 5) - 1

## Interpretation
Ratio of return volatility to average absolute return, minus 1. Measures kurtosis-like excess dispersion in recent returns.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
volatility
