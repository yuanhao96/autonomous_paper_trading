# WQ-127: Distance from 12-day high plus smoothed daily returns

## Formula
(close - ts_max(close, 12)) / ts_max(close, 12) + sma(delta(close, 1) / close, 5)

## Interpretation
Distance from 12-day high plus smoothed daily returns. Combination of drawdown depth and recovery momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| max_window | 12 | [6, 24] |
| return_sma | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
volatility
