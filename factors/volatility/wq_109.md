# WQ-109: Short-term average range relative to longer-term

## Formula
sma(high - low, 10) / sma(high - low, 50) - 1

## Interpretation
Short-term average range relative to longer-term. Positive when recent ranges are wider than usual -- volatility expansion.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| fast_window | 10 | [5, 20] |
| slow_window | 50 | [25, 100] |

## Category
volatility
