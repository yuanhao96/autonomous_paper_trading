# WQ-188: Today's range relative to average range

## Formula
(high - low) / sma(high - low, 20) - 1

## Interpretation
Today's range relative to average range. Positive on range expansion days -- potential breakout or trend day.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
volatility

WorldQuant Alpha#188 (Kakushadze 2015)
