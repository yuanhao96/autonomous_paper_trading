# WQ-078: 20-day Donchian channel position, centered at zero

## Formula
(close - ts_min(low, 20)) / (ts_max(high, 20) - ts_min(low, 20) + 1e-8) - 0.5

## Interpretation
20-day Donchian channel position, centered at zero. Positive values indicate the price is in the upper half of the channel (bullish); negative values indicate the lower half (bearish). Zero-centered for direct use as a signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
price_channel
