# WQ-072: Where close sits relative to the smoothed high/low channel

## Formula
(sma(high, 5) - close) / (sma(high, 5) - sma(low, 5) + 1e-8)

## Interpretation
Where close sits relative to the smoothed high/low channel. Near 0 means the close is near recent highs; near 1 means the close is near recent lows. A fast-reacting channel position indicator.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
price_channel
