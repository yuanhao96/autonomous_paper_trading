# WQ-126: Typical price (pivot point) minus its 20-day SMA

## Formula
(close + high + low) / 3 - sma((close + high + low) / 3, 20)

## Interpretation
Typical price (pivot point) minus its 20-day SMA. Measures pivot-point deviation from trend. Positive when the current pivot is above the average pivot -- indicates near-term bullishness.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
price_channel
