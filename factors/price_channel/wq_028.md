# WQ-028: Price position within the 12-day Donchian channel

## Formula
(close - ts_min(low, 12)) / (ts_max(high, 12) - ts_min(low, 12) + 1e-8)

## Interpretation
Price position within the 12-day Donchian channel. A value of 1.0 means the close is at the channel high; 0.0 means at the channel low. Acts as a breakout indicator -- values near 1.0 suggest upside breakout momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 12 | [6, 24] |

## Category
price_channel
