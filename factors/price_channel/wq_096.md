# WQ-096: Drawdown from 10-day high plus 3-day return

## Formula
ts_max(close, 10) / close - 1 + delta(close, 3) / delay(close, 3)

## Interpretation
Drawdown from 10-day high plus 3-day return. The negative drawdown component is offset by positive momentum recovery. Signals recovery from dips -- positive when the 3-day bounce exceeds the drawdown from the recent high.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| max_window | 10 | [5, 20] |
| return_window | 3 | [1, 6] |

## Category
price_channel
