# WQ-177: Distance from the 20-day channel top, normalized by chann...

## Formula
(ts_max(high, 20) - close) / (ts_max(high, 20) - ts_min(low, 20) + 1e-8)

## Interpretation
Distance from the 20-day channel top, normalized by channel width. Near 0 means close is near the high (bullish breakout territory); near 1 means close is near the low (bearish). Inverse of Alpha#078.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
price_channel
