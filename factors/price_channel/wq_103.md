# WQ-103: Distance from 20-day low as a percentage of current price

## Formula
(close - ts_min(low, 20)) / (close + 1e-8) * 100

## Interpretation
Distance from 20-day low as a percentage of current price. Large values indicate the price has rallied far from support -- potential resistance level or trend continuation depending on context.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
price_channel
