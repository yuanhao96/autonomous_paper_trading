# WQ-082: Boolean: did today's close exceed yesterday's 15-day high...

## Formula
delay(ts_max(high, 15), 1) < close

## Interpretation
Boolean: did today's close exceed yesterday's 15-day high? Returns true (1) on breakout days. Converts to a 1/0 signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 15 | [7, 30] |

## Category
price_channel
