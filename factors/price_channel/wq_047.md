# WQ-047: Distance from channel high, normalized by channel width, ...

## Formula
(ts_max(high, 6) - close) / (ts_max(high, 6) - ts_min(low, 6) + 1e-8) * volume / sma(volume, 20)

## Interpretation
Distance from channel high, normalized by channel width, scaled by relative volume. Combines channel position with volume confirmation. High values indicate the price is far from the channel top on above-average volume.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| channel_window | 6 | [3, 12] |
| vol_sma | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
price_channel
