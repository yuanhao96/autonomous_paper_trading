# WQ-090: Ranked serial correlation times ranked SMA deviation

## Formula
-1 * rank(correlation(delay(close, 1), close, 5)) * rank(close - sma(close, 20) / sma(close, 60))

## Interpretation
Product of (1) ranked 5-day serial correlation of close prices and (2) ranked deviation of close from the ratio of 20-day to 60-day SMA. Targets stocks with high autocorrelation that are overextended relative to multi-timeframe moving averages.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| corr_lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |
| short_sma | 20 | [10, 30] |
| long_sma | 60 | [40, 80] |

cross_sectional
