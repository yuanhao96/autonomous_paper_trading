# WQ-115: Product of two ranked correlations

## Formula
rank(correlation(high * 0.9 + close * 0.1, sma(volume, 30), 10)) * rank(correlation(ts_rank(high + low / 2, 3), ts_rank(volume, 10), 7))

## Interpretation
Product of two ranked correlation signals: (1) correlation between a weighted high-close blend and smoothed volume, and (2) correlation between time-series-ranked midpoint and time-series-ranked volume. Captures the alignment of price-volume relationships across multiple horizons.
