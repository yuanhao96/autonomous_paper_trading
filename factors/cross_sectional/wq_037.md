# WQ-037: Ranked long-term correlation plus ranked intraday return

## Formula
rank(correlation(delay(open - close, 1), close, 200)) + rank(open - close)

## Interpretation
Sum of two ranked signals: (1) the long-term correlation between lagged intraday return and close price, and (2) today's intraday return. Combines a structural relationship with a daily cross-sectional signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| corr_lookback | 200 | [100, 250] |

## Type
cross_sectional

cross_sectional
