# WQ-045: Triple-factor ranked product

## Formula
-1 * rank(sma(delay(close, 5), 20)) * correlation(close, volume, 2) * rank(correlation(sum(close, 5), sum(close, 20), 2))

## Interpretation
Product of three signals: (1) ranked smoothed lagged close, (2) short-term close-volume correlation, (3) ranked correlation between short and long cumulative close. Targets stocks where lagged price, volume confirmation, and multi-horizon alignment diverge.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| delay_d | 5 | [3, 10] |
| sma_d | 20 | [10, 30] |
| corr_d | 2 | [2, 5] |

cross_sectional
