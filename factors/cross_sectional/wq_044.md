# WQ-044: High vs ranked volume correlation

## Formula
-1 * correlation(high, rank(volume), 5)

## Interpretation
Negative correlation between high price and cross-sectionally ranked volume over 5 days. When a stock's high price moves with its relative volume ranking, this signal is negative (bearish).

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
