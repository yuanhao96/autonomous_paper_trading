# WQ-136: Ranked return acceleration times open-volume correlation

## Formula
rank(delta(returns, 3)) * correlation(open, volume, 10) * -1

## Interpretation
Product of (1) cross-sectionally ranked 3-day change in returns (return acceleration) and (2) 10-day correlation between open price and volume, negated. Targets stocks with accelerating returns where open-volume correlation is breaking down.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| delta_d | 3 | [2, 5] |
| corr_lookback | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
