# WQ-008: Ranked midpoint price acceleration

## Formula
-1 * rank(delta(close * 0.5 + open * 0.5, 4) - delta(close * 0.5 + open * 0.5, 8))

## Interpretation
Ranks the acceleration of the midpoint price (average of open and close). Negative sign means contrarian: stocks with the largest midpoint acceleration rank lowest. Captures mean reversion in price momentum.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| short_delta | 4 | [2, 6] |
| long_delta | 8 | [6, 12] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
