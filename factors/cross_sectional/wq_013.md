# WQ-013: Ranked close-volume rank covariance

## Formula
-1 * rank(covariance(rank(close), rank(volume), 5))

## Interpretation
Ranks the rolling covariance between cross-sectional ranks of close price and volume. When a stock's relative close and relative volume co-move, the covariance is high. Negative sign favors stocks where relative price and relative volume diverge.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
