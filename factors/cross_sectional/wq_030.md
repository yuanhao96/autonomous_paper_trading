# WQ-030: Ranked directional persistence times volume ratio

## Formula
rank(sign(close - delay(close, 1)) + sign(delay(close, 1) - delay(close, 2)) + sign(delay(close, 2) - delay(close, 3))) * sum(volume, 5) / sum(volume, 20)

## Interpretation
Sums the signs of the last 3 daily price changes (directional persistence: -3 to +3), ranks this across tickers, then multiplies by the short-term to long-term volume ratio. Identifies relatively persistent movers with volume confirmation.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| short_vol | 5 | [3, 10] |
| long_vol | 20 | [10, 40] |

cross_sectional
