# WQ-016: Ranked high-volume rank covariance

## Formula
-1 * rank(covariance(rank(high), rank(volume), 5))

## Interpretation
Same structure as Alpha#013 but uses high price instead of close. Ranks the rolling covariance between cross-sectional ranks of high and volume. Favors stocks where relative high and relative volume diverge.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
