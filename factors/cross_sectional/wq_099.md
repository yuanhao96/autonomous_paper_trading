# WQ-099: Ranked close-volume rank covariance (variant)

## Formula
-1 * rank(covariance(rank(close), rank(volume), 5))

## Interpretation
Identical structure to Alpha#013. Ranks the rolling covariance between cross-sectional ranks of close and volume. Negative sign favors stocks where relative price and relative volume movements diverge. Included as a robustness check.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |

## Type
cross_sectional

## Category
cross_sectional
