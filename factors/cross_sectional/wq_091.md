# WQ-091: Distance from 5-day max times ranked volume-low correlation

## Formula
-1 * rank(close - max(close, 5)) * rank(correlation(sma(volume, 40), low, 5))

## Interpretation
Product of (1) ranked distance of close from its 5-day high and (2) ranked correlation between smoothed volume and low price. Targets stocks near recent highs where volume and low price are correlated (potential distribution).

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| max_lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |
| vol_sma | 40 | [20, 60] |
| corr_lookback | 5 | [3, 10] |

## Type
cross_sectional
