# WQ-051: If 8 or more of the last 12 days were up (strong uptrend)...

## Formula
where(sum(where(delta(close, 1) > 0, 1, 0), 12) >= 8, delta(close, 1), -1 * delta(close, 1))

## Interpretation
If 8 or more of the last 12 days were up (strong uptrend), follow today's change. Otherwise, reverse it. Trend persistence conditional.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| count_window | 12 | [6, 24] |
| threshold | 8 | [4, 16] |

## Category
trend
