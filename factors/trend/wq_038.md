# WQ-038: Rank of intraday returns minus rank of close level

## Formula
-1 * ts_rank(close, 10) + ts_rank(close / open, 10)

## Interpretation
Rank of intraday returns minus rank of close level. Favors days where intraday performance is strong relative to overall price level positioning.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
trend
