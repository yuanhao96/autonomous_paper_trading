# WQ-050: Running average of up/down day indicators

## Formula
sma(where(delta(close, 1) > 0, 1, -1), 10)

## Interpretation
Running average of up/down day indicators. +1 for each up day, -1 for each down day, averaged over 10 days. Directional consistency measure.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
trend
