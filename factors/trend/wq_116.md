# WQ-116: Time-series rank of 10-day average return over 20 days

## Formula
ts_rank(sma(delta(close, 1) / delay(close, 1), 10), 20)

## Interpretation
Time-series rank of 10-day average return over 20 days. Measures where current smoothed momentum sits in its recent history.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| return_sma | 10 | [5, 20] |
| rank_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
trend
