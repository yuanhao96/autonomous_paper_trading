# WQ-093: Ratio of total up-moves to total down-moves over 15 days,...

## Formula
sum(where(delta(close, 1) > 0, delta(close, 1), 0), 15) / sum(where(delta(close, 1) < 0, abs(delta(close, 1)), 0), 15) - 1

## Interpretation
Ratio of total up-moves to total down-moves over 15 days, minus 1. Positive when up-move magnitude exceeds down-moves -- bullish directional bias.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 15 | [7, 30] |

## Category
trend
