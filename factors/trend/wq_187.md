# WQ-187: Win rate (fraction of up days) over 20 days, centered at 0

## Formula
sum(where(delta(close, 1) > 0, 1, 0), 20) / 20 - 0.5 + sma(delta(close, 1), 10) / close

## Interpretation
Win rate (fraction of up days) over 20 days, centered at 0.5, plus smoothed daily change. Combines directional consistency with trend magnitude.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| count_window | 20 | [10, 40] |
| sma_window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
trend
