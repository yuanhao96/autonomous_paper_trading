# WQ-021: Price deviation from 8-day SMA plus smoothed daily change...

## Formula
sma(close, 8) / close - 1 + sma(delta(close, 1), 8) / close

## Interpretation
Price deviation from 8-day SMA plus smoothed daily change normalized by price. Combines trend position with trend velocity.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 8 | [4, 16] |
| delta_sma | 8 | [4, 16] |

## Category
trend
