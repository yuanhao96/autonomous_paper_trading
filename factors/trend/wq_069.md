# WQ-069: 5-day rate of change of the 5-day SMA

## Formula
delta(sma(close, 5), 5) / delay(sma(close, 5), 5)

## Interpretation
5-day rate of change of the 5-day SMA. Smoothed trend acceleration -- positive when the moving average is rising.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| roc_window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
trend
