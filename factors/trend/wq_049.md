# WQ-049: Sum of absolute up and down moves (always positive)

## Formula
where(delta(close, 1) > 0, delta(close, 1), 0) - where(delta(close, 1) < 0, delta(close, 1), 0)

## Interpretation
Sum of absolute up and down moves (always positive). Simplifies to `abs(delta(close, 1))`. Measures total daily movement magnitude regardless of direction.

## Parameters
None

## Category
trend
