# WQ-023: If today's high exceeds the 20-day average high (overexte...

## Formula
where(sma(high, 20) < high, -1 * delta(high, 2), 0)

## Interpretation
If today's high exceeds the 20-day average high (overextension), short the 2-day high change. Mean reversion from overbought highs.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 20 | [10, 40] |
| delta_days | 2 | [1, 4] |

## Category
mean_reversion
