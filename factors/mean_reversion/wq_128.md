# WQ-128: When price is in the top 30% of its 14-day range (overbou...

## Formula
where((close - ts_min(close, 14)) / (ts_max(close, 14) - ts_min(close, 14) + 1e-8) > 0.7, -1 * delta(close, 3) / close, delta(close, 3) / close)

## Interpretation
When price is in the top 30% of its 14-day range (overbought), flip the 3-day return signal. RSI-style reversal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| range_window | 14 | [7, 28] |
| overbought_threshold | 0.7 | [0.35, 1.4] |
| return_window | 3 | [1, 6] |

## Category
mean_reversion
