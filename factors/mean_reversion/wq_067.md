# WQ-067: Stochastic oscillator variant over 24 days, centered at zero

## Formula
(close - ts_min(close, 24)) / (ts_max(close, 24) - ts_min(close, 24) + 1e-8) - 0.5

## Interpretation
Stochastic oscillator variant over 24 days, centered at zero. Above 0 = upper half of range, below 0 = lower half.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 24 | [12, 48] |

## Category
mean_reversion
