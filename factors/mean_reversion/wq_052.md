# WQ-052: Difference between the rate of change of 5-day lows and 5...

## Formula
delta(ts_min(low, 5), 5) / 5 - delta(close, 5) / 5

## Interpretation
Difference between the rate of change of 5-day lows and 5-day close change. Positive when lows are rising faster than closes -- reversion from oversold.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 5 | [2, 10] |

## Category
mean_reversion
