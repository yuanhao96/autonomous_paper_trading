# WQ-112: Inverted z-score (positive when price is below average)

## Formula
(sma(close, 12) - close) / (stddev(close, 12) + 1e-8)

## Interpretation
Inverted z-score (positive when price is below average). Direct mean reversion -- buy when cheap relative to recent range.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 12 | [6, 24] |

## Category
mean_reversion
