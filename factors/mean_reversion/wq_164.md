# WQ-164: Negative z-score of the price-to-SMA ratio

## Formula
-1 * (close / sma(close, 20) - 1) / stddev(close / sma(close, 20), 20)

## Interpretation
Negative z-score of the price-to-SMA ratio. Strong mean reversion: sells when price is extended above average, buys when below.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| sma_window | 20 | [10, 40] |
| std_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
mean_reversion
