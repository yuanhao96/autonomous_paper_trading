# WQ-110: Average normalized range minus current normalized range

## Formula
sma(high - low, 10) / close - (high - low) / close

## Interpretation
Average normalized range minus current normalized range. Positive when current range is narrow (contraction -- expect expansion).

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| window | 10 | [5, 20] |

## Category
mean_reversion

## Source
WorldQuant Alpha#110 (Kakushadze 2015)
