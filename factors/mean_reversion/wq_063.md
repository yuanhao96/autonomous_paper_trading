# WQ-063: Smoothed deviation from 20-day mean, normalized by volati...

## Formula
sma(close - sma(close, 20), 5) / stddev(close, 20)

## Interpretation
Smoothed deviation from 20-day mean, normalized by volatility. Z-score-like mean reversion signal.

## Parameters
| Param | Default | Range |
|-------|---------|-------|
| mean_window | 20 | [10, 40] |
| smooth_window | 5 | [2, 10] |
| std_window | 20 | [10, 40] |

## Category
mean_reversion
